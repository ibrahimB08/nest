Mission

To Enhance the health and well-being of communities especially Children, Women and youth through the delivery of innovative, results-oriented, Social, Emotional, Physical and cognitive development.

Vision

To catalyze constant positive transformation in the lives of people, especially vulnerable children, youth and women through equitable opportunity and comprehensive development to lead a dignified life in the society.
